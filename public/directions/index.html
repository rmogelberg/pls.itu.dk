Getting to ITU by Public Transportation

The ITU is located between the metro stations Islands Brygge and DR Byen. Tickets for public transportation (metro, trains, buses) can be bought via the Rejsekort app.

Within the Building

Our group is located on the fourth floors in the “C” wing. You can find 4C in the north east corner of the building.
